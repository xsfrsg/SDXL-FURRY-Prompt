- Subject type: person (anime-stylized girl in sketchy line art)

- Age: 10–12; 
Vibe: fragile / solemn / faintly ominous; 
Skin: pale graphite; 
Makeup: none;

- Outfit: simple black long-sleeve dress with small white collar + thin pale hands;
